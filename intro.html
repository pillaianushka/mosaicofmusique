The Mosaic of Music – The Tale Begins

The Prince from Another Realm

In a world where melodies paint the skies and rhythm fuels the soul, our hero is a young prince from a distant realm. He is suddenly torn away from his homeland. He awakens in a strange, fractured world, where the once-vibrant nations lie silent. The soulless people of Obscuria have drained creativity from every corner, replacing it with mechanical perfection.

The prince’s mission is clear: to retrieve the five lost Shards of Harmony scattered across the kingdoms. Only by restoring their light can he save this world, and regain his lost honor to return home.

The Five Kingdoms of Sound

Each kingdom represents a genre of music, a fragment of creativity itself. Together, they form the great Mosaic of Musique, the source of all art and emotion in this realm.

Elandria

Land of grand halls and golden orchestras, where wisdom echoes in every note. The Elandrian shard glows softly, a gemstone of harmony that keeps tradition alive.

~Classical, Opera, Baroque, Instrumental~

Ondaria

Home to free spirits, smooth rhythms, and endless improvisation. The Ondarians believe life itself is a song, ever-changing and never bound by rules.

~Jazz, Reggae, Blues, Funk, Soul~

Silexia

Forged in passion and rebellion, Silexia’s people stand tall against silence. Their shard pulses like a beating heart, fierce, alive, and unstoppable.

~Rock, Metal, Punk, Indie, Alt~

Szletnia

Rhythmic streets and poetic souls define this kingdom. In every rhyme and beat lies truth, strength, and unity in the voice of the people.

~Hip Hop, Rap, R&B, Trap~

Cantoria

Bright, dazzling, and ever-evolving, Cantoria is where dreams and melodies collide. The shard of Cantoria shines with joy, a reminder of the beauty of connection.

~Pop, EDM, K-pop, Disco, Synthwave~

The Soulless Machine

From the depths of Obscuria arises the Soulless Machinewho is a cold, calculating entity who believes perfection lies in logic, not emotion. It seeks to erase all creativity, replacing it with flawless, machine-produced art devoid of soul or heart.

To protect the remaining sparks of imagination and creativity, the shards were hidden away across the five kingdoms. But their power wanes... and time is running out.

Only by solving each kingdom’s challenges, aiding the characters, and reviving the hope in their hearts can the prince bring the shards back to life.

The Journey Awaits

This is not just a quest for redemption, it is a battle for the soul of art itself. From ancient ruins to neon-lit cities, from whispers of violins to echoes of drums, the prince must journey through the kingdoms shaped by sound and silence alike.

The choices you make will shape his destiny.
Will harmony prevail... or will the colourless void consume all? Note: You can start with any kingdom and collect all the shards to move on to Obscuria
