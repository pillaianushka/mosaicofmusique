Symphony of Realms

Project Overview – Music, Adventure, and Interactive Storytelling

Introduction

Symphony of Realms is an immersive music-themed adventure game where players traverse five unique kingdoms: Pop, Rock, Classical, Jazz, and Hip-Hop. Each kingdom presents challenges, puzzles, and musical riddles that test the player’s memory, logic, and creativity.

About the Game Lore

The protagonist, a prince from a distant realm, has been transported to a world under threat by soulless AI entities. These entities have stolen the harmony of music, spreading monotony and chaos. The player’s mission is to restore balance by retrieving music shards hidden across the kingdoms.

Our Goals

  • Combine gaming, music education, and storytelling.
  • Create an interactive experience that encourages problem-solving and memory skills.
  • Promote musical awareness and creativity.

A Peek into the World

Players encounter visually distinct kingdoms, each inspired by a musical genre. The environments, riddles, and rewards reflect the character of the kingdom and challenge players in different ways.

Our Work

We focused on game design, level creation, coding mechanics, and integrating music and audio feedback. The project also includes interactive elements like memory games, puzzles, and thematic storytelling.

Levels and Examples

  • Level 1: Song of Shards (Obscuria) – Match shards to their theme songs.
  • Level 2: The Forgotten Chamber (Classical) – Memory game using musical instruments.
  • Level 3: Tunnels of Szletnia (Hip-Hop) – Flappy Bird-style navigation challenge.

Conclusion

Symphony of Realms showcases creativity in game design and emphasizes the importance of music and problem-solving. The project highlights our ability to integrate narrative, interactive design, and programming.
